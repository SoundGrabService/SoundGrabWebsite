Privacy Policy for SoundGrab

Last updated: December 7, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to SoundGrab ("we", "our", or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Downloaded Content Metadata: Track titles, artists, and playlists (stored locally on your device)
  • App Preferences: Theme settings, audio quality preferences

2.2 Automatically Collected Data

  • Ad Interaction Data: When ads are enabled, Google AdMob collects data about ad views and interactions
  • App Analytics: Crash reports and performance data to improve the app
  • Device Information: Operating system version, device model (for compatibility)

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide core app functionality (downloading and playing music)
  • To save your preferences across app sessions
  • To display relevant advertisements (when ads are enabled)
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs

4. Data Storage

Local Storage: All downloaded music files and app data are stored exclusively on your device. We do not store your files on external servers.

No Cloud Upload: Your downloads are never uploaded to our servers or any third-party services.

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and process data to show personalized ads.

  • AdMob Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Ad Personalization Control: https://adssettings.google.com/

5.2 SoundCloud

SoundGrab interacts with SoundCloud's public APIs to fetch track information. We do not collect or store your SoundCloud credentials.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties, except:

  • With Google AdMob for advertising purposes (as described above)
  • When required by law or legal process

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about data we collect
  • Delete: Uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data
  • Opt-out: Disable ad personalization via Google settings

8. Children's Privacy

SoundGrab is not intended for users under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at:

Email: support.soundgrab@proton.me
